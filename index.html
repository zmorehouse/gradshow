About Gradshow '23'

On behalf of the Australian National University, School of Art & Design, I am delighted to share with you the work of our 2023 graduating cohort, spanning art history and curatorship, design, and visual arts.

Excitedly, the 2022 Graduating Exhibition is now open to public! This exhibition marks a huge achievement, despite the challenges of the past few years.

The School is incredibly proud of the works of all our students over 2023.

Support Our Graduates

There are a number of ways you can support our 2023 graduates. Outside attending the exhibiton, there are a handful of ways to help out, including buying artworks and donating to our various artists support schemes.
